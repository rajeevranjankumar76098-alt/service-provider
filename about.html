About Service Provider Centre

Service Provider Centre (SPC) is a trusted online platform that connects customers with professional technicians for all types of repair and maintenance services. We aim to make home and electronic repairs simple, fast, and reliable by providing doorstep services across India.

Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver high-quality repair services with complete transparency, affordable pricing, and timely support. We focus on customer satisfaction by ensuring skilled technicians, genuine spare parts, and quick service.

Our Vision

To become India’s most trusted and largest service platform for repair and maintenance services, empowering technicians and providing hassle-free solutions to every household.
